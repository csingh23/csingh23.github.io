Spreading Education, One Book at a Time

The World Book Bank Club relies on a community-based effort to collect books. Your book donations could go a long way toward providing an education to children in underdeveloped countries.

Learn more

Background

The World Book Bank Club is an initiative to gather quality books that will make a difference in the lives of children in developing countries.

As members of the club, we are committed to also organizing, sorting, and packaging these books. In conjunction with The World Bank and its Family Network Book Project, these books are then shipped to underfunded schools in countries across the globe.
